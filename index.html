To harness the power of the sun

We need a Sun box.

We are building something to hold the energy of a star. It is being built right now in France. A global effort. It is a tokamak. An experimental machine.

Fuelled by seawater. It will not produce greenhouse gases. Fossil fuel use is harming the environment. Fusion could become a replacement for fossil fuel.

The radioactive waste it does produce will be fit for reuse after 100 years. Some nuclear industry waste will be dangerous for 1000 to 10,000 years.

Fusion is so difficult to achieve that the United States, Russia, the EU, South Korea, Japan and India came to together to build the tokamak in the south of France. It will take 10 years to build. And run for 20.
